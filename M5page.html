The BMW M5 competition

The new BMW M5 competition combines circuit characteristics with ultimate driving pleasure for daily driving, which is a characteristic for all BMW M models. The 4.4-liter V8 twinturbocharged engine produces 625 horsepower. This makes the M5 accelarate from 0 to 100kph in just 3.2 (2.88) seconds. Just like the reactionspeed of the engine is the sound of a standard fitted M sport exhaust customizable with a flip of the switch. The driver can adjust the loudness and sportiness of the exhaust at any moment. The driving force is fitted with an 8-step M steptronic transmission with drivelogic systems. The standard equipment contains exclusive 20-inch forged alloy M rims with various tire sizes. The purchase prices start from 161500 euros.
